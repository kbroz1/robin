I'm Robin...






…I’m from California and I love dance. Really, I’m obsessed with it. I think dance is both incredibly expansive and introspective, and an ability we are all born with. I’ve been training in dance for 20 years in a variety of styles. I currently live in Brooklyn, NY but study (online) with London Contemporary Dance School. My goal and mission as a dancer, artist, and teacher is to make dance as physically, emotionally, and financially accessible as possible. I value honest, courageous and brave movement and strive to build and deliver classes that encourage people to take chances, try new things, and let go. If you’re interested to know more about me, send me an email! I’m open to answer any questions you may have about me or my classes. I hope to dance with you soon!
